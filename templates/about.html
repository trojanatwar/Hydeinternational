About Us

Hyde International (UK) is a bespoke international cross-cultural solution and opportunity provider for organisations and talents between Europe and China who are dedicated to creating changes that matter.

Value
  • Integrity & Partnership   
  • Agility & Diversity   
  • Possibility & Responsibility

Integrity & Partnership We work as one global team, both with each other and our clients, to direct our collective energy towards achieving the extraordinary.
Agility & Diversity We aim to adapt and respond to change with flexibility and intellectual acuity and respect for all individuals.
Possibility & Responsibility We embrace innovation and remain open to new possibilities. We measure our success by our client's sustainable success, and we believe we can make a better world.
